Penn Badgley

I’m Penn Badgley, born on November 1, 1986, in Baltimore, Maryland. I'm best known for my roles as Dan Humphrey on "Gossip Girl" and Joe Goldberg on Netflix's "You." Apart from acting, I’m the lead singer of the indie band MOTHXR, which released our debut album "Centerfold" in 2016. I'm also passionate about social and environmental issues. I married singer Domino Kirke in 2017.

Latest Work

You

You is a psychological thriller where I play Joe Goldberg, a charming but disturbed bookstore manager who becomes dangerously obsessed with his love interests. The show explores themes of obsession, privacy, and the dark side of modern romance.
